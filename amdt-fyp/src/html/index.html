  • Welcome to the Blue Shadow App!

  • Fallow these steps to generate your SCSS color variable file!
  • Choose your Ps files or Ai files from the level 1 folder.
  • Click enabled "Upload Files" button on right top corner to upload your files to the application.
  • Ps Ai Ps Ai Ps Ps Uploading...
  • Now you have your level 1 file names as the variables.
  • Assign the values of your .psd files or .ai files to this variables. You can assign HEX/RGBA/Linear Gradient/Radial Gradient/URL using the drop down of each variable.
    Please check the correct values which you set on your .psd or .ai files. Until your project look will crash.
  • Then click enabled "Next" button on left bottom corner go to the level 2.
  • Choose your Ps files or Ai files from the level 2 folder.
  • Then Click enabled "Upload Files" button on right top corner to upload your files to the application.
  • Ps Ai Ps Ai Ps Ps Uploading...
  • Now you have your level 2 file names as the variables.
  • Assign the values of level 1 files to this variables. You can assign the level 1 values using the drop down of each variable.
    Please check the correct variable which you set on level 1. Until your project look will crash.
  • Click "Generate SCSS" button on left bottom corner go to the last level

  • Your SCSS color variables file successfully generated!

  • Click "Copy SCSS" button on left bottom corner to copy this file.
